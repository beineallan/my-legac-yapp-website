Many mobile money accounts become deactivated over a certain period of time due to inactivity. And sometimes these reasons of inactivity are beyond the control of the user or owner of the mobile money account. This could be death or disability of the user. So in this unfortunate case what happens to the users’ money/balance is sometimes unknown. Our application is to provide a solution to this un-certainty. We have thus decided to name it the ‘MY LEGACY PROJECT’.

Available Platforms

Upon completion of login, and verification of the user’s credentials/details, the application provides a menu list of various ‘soft cash’ accounts. For now, we are initially providing a platform for MTN mobile money, Airtel Money, Visa and 3 types of Crypto currency as illustrated in the application’s screenshot below:

After the user has selected the type of account to which he/she wishes to appoint inheritor(s), the application will provide a page for entering the inheritor(s)’ details for submission and verification. The user can add as many as five inheritors to any specific account, each with a varying or equal amount as he/she prefers by tapping on the plus/add button as seen at the bottom of the screen above.
